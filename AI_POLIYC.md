# AI policy

This repository does not permit the use of generative AI systems in the creation of code, documentation, tests, issues pull, requests or other project contributions.

The goal of this policy is to ensure that all contributions are fully human authored, fully understood by their authors, and free from licensing, attribution and originality concerns associated with AI generated slopware.

---

# Prohibited tools

The following categories of tools may not be used to make contributions to the parent repository

- Large language models (LLMs) local or web
- AI coding assistants
- AI text generators
- AI summarization systems
- AI refactoring systems

Examples include but are not limited to

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Deepseek
- Google Gemini
- Google Bard
- GitHub Copilot
- Cursor AI
- Replit AI
- Some new hot AI in the town
- Codex (but not the Umbra)

This restriction applies whether the AI output is used directly, paraphrased, edited, summarized, translated or rewritten.

---

# Contribution requirements

By contributing to this repository you confirm that:

- You personally understood and could explain ALL submitted work
- Your work does not contain AI-generated material.

The maintainers may

- request clarification regarding contribution authorship
- reject contributions suspected of AI generation without notice
- remove contributions found to violate this policy
- ban repeat offenders from contributing after a warning

Submitters are responsible for ensuring that all submitted material

- is original work
- is compatible with the repository license
- does not include AI-generated content
